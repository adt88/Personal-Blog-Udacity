Hi, my name is Alexis Thomas and I am traveler to Europe 7 times. I am writing this blog because I wanted to showcase my one of my favorite trips, Which is when I went to Italy with some of my closest friends.

About my Trip

This was a nine day trip that began in Rome, where we spent three days and visit such sites like the Vatican, Coloseum, and the Trevi Fountain.

The second leg of the trip we to a train from Rome to Florence. There we spent three days visiting such sites as the Duomo, The Leaning Tower of Pisa, Cinque Terra, and a plethora of museums.

The last leg to the trip was to Venice. Here we rode gondolas, visited historic sites and enjoy fine cusine.

All in the trip was amazing, this was truly a wonder place to visit and memories that I can have for a lifetime.
